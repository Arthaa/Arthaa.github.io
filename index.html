RULES

Rules and Regulations should be followed by everyone:-

1. Carry your college/school ID at the time of registration. The ID issued by team ARTHA should also be carried at all times. You won’t be allowed to participate in any event if ID card is not possessed.

2. Those availing accommodation have to enter the campus by 7 pm and their allotted hostels by 8 pm.

3. Consumption of alcohol and other related intoxicants is strictly prohibited in the campus. Defaulters will be reported to the security and strict action will be taken against them.

4. No vehicles are allowed in the campus.

5. Boys are not allowed in girl’s hostel and vice versa.

.

6. Any kind of nuisance or unruly behavior won't be tolerated.

7. Candidate found violating the stated rules and regulations would be debarred from the campus. Strict action would be taken against the involved college contingent and may result in disqualification of the team.

SCRUTINIZE THE DEPARTMENT

The University has a diverse extent of which one extent is “The School of Economics”.

The School of Economics at Shri Mata Vaishno Devi University, Katra (J&K) is a newly established School under Faculty of Management. The mission of the School is to build up international level centre of excellence in Economic studies with innovative research while creating and disseminating knowledge in a global society. The vision is to rank among the best Schools of economics in the world. In present scenario of Global Economic Development, there are immense requirement of Human Resources in various fields. The Higher study in economics is a special choice in career option to reach prestigious future target in career. By studying Economics, the students can use their talent to eradicate poverty and make this world a better place to live in.

EVENT 1:

FUTURE CHANAKYA

For Schools and Colleges

DESCRIPTION:

This event will have 3 rounds.

ROUND 1:

The students will be given some highlights of present buget at the time of registration. They have to speak on any one topic within the given highlights. 2 members from each sschool can participate and out of total participation top 50% will go to the next round.

TIME LIMIT:

2 to 3 minutes for each round.

ROUND 2: DEBATE

TOPIC: Should Goverment Subsidize Farmers?

Students will be asked to speak either for or against the motion.

TIME LIMIT:

2 minutes

ROUND 3: GROUP DISCUSSION

TOPIC: Environmental Degradation a Repercussion of Tourism Upgradation.

In this round students will be evaluated on the basis of facts and figures they present, the body language, communicative skills, postures, hoe to retain their balance, relevance, etc.

  • Date: 16th of February 2018

EVENT 6:

SHIPWRECK

Only for Universities and Colleges

You are given the role of Narendra Modi,Arun Jately etc. the ship is sinking and the caption has only one life jacket left. you are on the ship with other famous celebrities, fictional characters, hypothetical characters. Sink into the given character and try to escape from the sinking ship by using your impressive, convincing skills and prove that you deserve the only life jacket. Make yourself appear prominent and better than any other person on board to win the life jacket. The participants are judged on content, humour, arguments, valid points.

TIME LIMIT:

3 minutes.
